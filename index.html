Ready to Celebrate?

Work With Us

01

Fill Out an Inquiry Form

Tell us about your event, guest count, preferred dates, and any special requests. We'll review all the details to ensure we're the perfect fit for your celebration.

02

We Reach Out & Discuss Details

We'll contact you via your preferred method — phone call, email, or text. We'll discuss the specifics of your event, including your unique flavor preferences, topping selections, and any custom designs you want to add to your jars.

03

Leave a Deposit to Secure Your Date

Once you're ready to move forward, a deposit will secure your event date on our calendar. This ensures your celebration is locked in and our team can begin planning.

04

Receive Custom Sample & Approval

For custom packages, we'll send you a picture sample of your designs 2 weeks before your event. Review and approve to make sure everything is exactly as you envisioned it.

05

Celebrate With Your Guests

Get ready to celebrate! We'll arrive with our fully loaded cake jar bar, craft and package custom cake jars on-site, and add a fun and interactive dessert experience to your event. Your guests leave with tasty take-homes!

Our Story

About the Founder

I've always loved creating experiences
that bring people together.

When I started dreaming about The Cake Jar Bar, I didn't just want another dessert business. I wanted to create something interactive, memorable, and fun.

Something that turns dessert into part of the celebration.

The Cake Jar Bar was created to help hosts simplify their events while giving guests an experience they'll remember. Instead of a traditional dessert table and separate party favors, guests can create their own gourmet cake jars and even take them home.

This is more than cake.

It's connection.
It's celebration.
It's creating sweet memories together.
